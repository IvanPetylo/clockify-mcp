## User Responsibilities

Users are responsible for ensuring they have permission to connect their Clockify account and use the exposed actions. Users must not submit API keys or data that they are not authorized to use.

## Service Scope

The first release is limited to personal time-tracking workflows: reading Clockify profile and work data, managing timers, creating or updating time entries, deleting time entries with explicit confirmation, and summarizing reports.

ClockifyMCP does not provide team administration, billing, HR, payroll, or organization management features.

## Credential Handling

ClockifyMCP validates the Clockify API key provided during onboarding and stores it encrypted. Users can request credential deletion through the service disconnect path or by contacting petylo.work+mcp@gmail.com.
